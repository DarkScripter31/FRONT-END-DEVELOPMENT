Rules to be followed:

  • 11 members per team plus 4 subs (optional).
  • 12-over bowling match.
  • No leg byes and LBW.
  • Red tennis ball will be used.
  • 2 bowlers can bowl a maximum of 3 overs each.
  • Each team has to bring their own bat and one new ball.
  • 4 overs powerplay.
  • Umpire's decision is final.
  • Every team should report 15 minutes in advance.
  • Each team should send 30 rupees for umpire refreshments.
  • Bowling team should complete the overs within the given time, or the same number of overs will be played by the opponent team.
